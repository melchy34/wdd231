Website Plan: EcoFoodie Recipes

Site Name

EcoFoodie Recipes

This name reflects a sustainable lifestyle-focused cooking website targeting eco-conscious food lovers who want recipes using local, organic, and plant-based ingredients.

Optional domain availability (not required): ecofoodie-recipes.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a curated collection of eco-friendly, plant-based recipes, tips on sustainable cooking, seasonal ingredient guides, and a community space for users to share and rate their own recipes. It promotes healthy living and mindful eating habits with an environmental focus.

Scenarios

  • What are some quick, sustainable meals I can prepare during the week?
  • How do I substitute common animal-based ingredients in baking?

Color Schema

  • Teal Green (#2a9d8f): Used for all main headings and navigation bars to convey freshness and nature.
  • Coral (#e76f51): Used for accents such as buttons, links, and icons to add a vibrant, modern touch.
  • Background (#f0f8f5): A soft off-white with a green tint used for page background to enhance readability and comfort.

Typography

  • Headings: Helvetica Neue, sans-serif – Clean and modern, ensures high readability and elegance.
  • Body: Arial, sans-serif – Simple and accessible for all browsers and devices.

Wireframe (Sketch Description)

Mobile View:

  • Top navigation hamburger menu
  • Logo centered
  • Main hero image with call-to-action (e.g., “Explore Recipes”)
  • Scrolling vertical sections:
    • Featured Recipes
    • Seasonal Tips
    • User Submissions

Desktop View:

  • Horizontal navigation bar at the top with logo on the left and menu on the right
  • Wide hero banner with a short intro
  • Three-column section: Recipes | Tips | Reviews
  • Footer with newsletter sign-up and social media links
