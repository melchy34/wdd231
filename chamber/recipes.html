Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)

Instructions: Toast the bread, mash the avocado, season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes. Spread and serve.

Berry Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Serve chilled.

Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Lemon juice and olive oil

Instructions: Mix all ingredients. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Chill before serving.
