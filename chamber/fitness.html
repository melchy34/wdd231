Fitness for Beginners

Discover simple and effective workouts to improve your health and energy. Whether you're just starting out or looking to stay active, these tips will help you reach your goals.

Easy At-Home Workout Routine

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio (jumping jacks, walking in place)
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8 reps (modify on knees if needed)
  • Plank: Hold for 20 seconds, repeat 3 times
  • Cool-down: Stretch major muscle groups for 5 minutes

Remember to listen to your body and progress at your own pace.

Benefits of Regular Exercise

Regular physical activity can improve your mood, boost energy, enhance sleep quality, and reduce risks of chronic diseases. Start small, stay consistent, and celebrate your progress!
